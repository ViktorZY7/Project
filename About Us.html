About Us

At the Sadhna Center, we focus on helping you become the best version of yourself. From yoga to weight loss and acupuncture to IV therapy, the Sadhna Center is the first facility of its kind: devoted to detecting, preventing, and treating age-related diseases. Based on the ancient concept of Sadhna and the years of research and work by Dr. Varma, the Sadhna Center is the place to come to gain a centered outlook, work on total body and mind well-being, and fight the signs of aging.

What Is Sadhna?

Sadhna, which translates to "a means to accomplish something," focuses on the continuous nurturing of your life, mind, and body. It's more than feeling good, it's more than taking care of yourself, it's using everything in your life as a tool to better your personal well-being.

Who Is Dr. Varma?

Vikas Varma has trained at some of the best teaching hospitals in the U.S including Mt. Sinai School of medicine, Hahnemann University, and North Shore University.
